About me

I'm a Software Engineer at Igalia with a PhD in Computer Science and 10+ years building systems. My work focuses on program verification, compilers, and static analysis. I'm a committer to LLVM and WebKit, and actively contribute to projects like ESBMC, CBMC, and Z3.

Recently, I've been focusing on RISC-V architecture optimization and serve as the maintainer of LLVM's libc RISC-V port.

I still write research papers in my free time and maintain an active interest in formal methods. My goal is making robust verification tools and compiler optimizations accessible to developers building reliable, high-performance software.
